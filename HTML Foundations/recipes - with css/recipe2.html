So for this project, I needed to finish a simple website which contained a recipe of something I like(who doesn't like lasagna, huh?).

Let's go for it, then. here is the recipe:


Ingredients


  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonigs
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to make it

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
